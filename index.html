Perform NYC

About Us

Our mission is to promote the merging of technology and the performing art. We hope to use the performing arts as a platform to expose and engage performers to visual arts technology. We want to break the mindset that creativity or technology is mutually exclusive.

Services

We have dance classes for technologists and technology classes for performers to help blend and create a bridge between the two sectors. We have performances in the community to showcase our programs and engage new learners who are interested in performance or technology. We have access to performance agencies, theaters, and major tech companies.
